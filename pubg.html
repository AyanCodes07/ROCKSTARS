PUBG

Game Overview

PUBG (PlayerUnknown's Battlegrounds) is an online multiplayer battle royale game that drops 100 players onto an island where they fight to be the last one standing. It combines survival, strategic looting, and combat in a large-scale open-world map.

Storyline

While PUBG doesn’t have a traditional narrative, it immerses players in a post-war setting where survival is the only goal. Players parachute down, scavenge weapons and supplies, and engage in intense combat until only one team or player remains.
