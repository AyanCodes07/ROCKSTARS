Call of Duty: Warzone

Game Overview

Call of Duty: Warzone is a free-to-play battle royale and large-scale combat experience developed by Infinity Ward and Raven Software, published by Activision. Set in the COD universe, Warzone offers both classic battle royale gameplay and unique modes like Plunder, Resurgence, and DMZ, featuring fast-paced tactical combat with stunning visuals.

Storyline

Warzone is built around Verdansk, a fictional Eastern European city linked with the Modern Warfare narrative. Players are dropped into zones ravaged by global warfare where factions like Armistice and Shadow Company battle for control. Over time, story events like zombie invasions and nuclear destruction evolve the map and game mechanics.
