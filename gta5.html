GTA 5

Game Overview

Grand Theft Auto V (GTA 5) is an open-world action-adventure game developed by Rockstar North and published by Rockstar Games. It is set in the fictional state of San Andreas, which is heavily inspired by Southern California. The game offers players an expansive world to explore on foot or by vehicle, and allows players to switch between three protagonists to experience the story from multiple perspectives.

Storyline

The story revolves around three criminals: Michael De Santa (a retired bank robber), Franklin Clinton (a street hustler), and Trevor Philips (a psychopathic drug dealer and gunrunner). Each of them has unique skills and missions, and their stories intertwine as they commit a series of heists while under pressure from corrupt government agents and dangerous criminals. The plot is rich with action, betrayal, humor, and classic Rockstar-style satire on American culture.
